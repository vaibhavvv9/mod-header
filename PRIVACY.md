# HeaderPilot Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 10, 2026**

HeaderPilot does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any user data.

- The header names and values you configure are stored locally in your
  browser via `chrome.storage.local`. They never leave your machine.
- The extension makes no network requests of its own and contains no
  analytics, telemetry, or third-party code.
- Header modification is performed entirely by Chrome's built-in
  `declarativeNetRequest` engine from rules stored in your browser.
- No personal information is accessed, read, or processed. The extension
  cannot read page content or request/response bodies.

The `<all_urls>` host permission exists solely so the headers **you**
configure can be applied to the sites you use them on.

Questions: open an issue at https://github.com/vaibhavvv9/mod-header/issues
